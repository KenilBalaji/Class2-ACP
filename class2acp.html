About Us

Shana K Wortham Intermediate School is committed to providing quality education and fostering a love for learning. Our school offers a wide range of academic and extracurricular programs to help students grow and succeed. It containes a diverse student body and a dedicated staff that works tirelessly to create a supportive and engaging learning environment. We believe in the importance of community involvement and encourage parents and guardians to participate in school activities for the benefit of our students. Our mission is to empower students to reach their full potential and become responsible, productive citizens. Ecspecially, that we have only 5th and 6th grade, we focus on the transition from elementary to middle school, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the challenges ahead.
